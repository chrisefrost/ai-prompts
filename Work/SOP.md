######ChatGPT

interview me one question at a time, in order to create a Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) for [specific process, e.g., handling customer returns]. Ensure the document includes:

1. A clear purpose and scope of the process.
2. Step-by-step instructions broken down into logical stages.
3. Roles and responsibilities for each task.
4. Any necessary tools, templates, or references.
5. Quality control checks to maintain consistency.
6. Contingency plans for exceptions or challenges.

The SOP should be easy to follow, adaptable for training, and written in professional yet accessible language.
